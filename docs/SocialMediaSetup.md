## Step-by-Step Account Setup

### Before You Start
- Have www.oahuunlocked.com open in a tab
- Have your profile image ready (logo or tight Hawaii aerial crop)

## Cross-Platform Rules

- **Tone:** Specific beats superlative. "Best poke on the island" is weak. "The ahi is caught that morning and the spicy mayo is not what you think" earns follows.
- **Always tag location** on every post — puts you in location search results for free
- **Pre-load before going public:** 6–9 posts on Instagram/TikTok, 10+ pins per board on Pinterest
- **Hashtag sweet spot:** 8–12 on Instagram (never more than 15), fewer on TikTok, keywords matter more than hashtags on Pinterest
- **Use #OahuUnlocked on every single post** — trains the algorithm and makes it searchable
- **Link Facebook Page to Instagram** via Meta Business Suite — manage both from one place
